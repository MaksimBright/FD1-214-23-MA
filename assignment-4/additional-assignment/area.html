Area and Map tags
Area is a tag that allow to take specific coordinates within image in order to make them clickable. This tag only used with map tag. Map in it's turn encapsulate area tags. Forms supported are circle/rectangle/polygon. For circle, you have to specify width, high of the center and radius. For rectangle, you have to specify top left corner width and high and same for bottom right corner. For polygon shape you have to specify each point of the shape you want.
